# Intervention Fidelity Rubric

**General Instructions:**  
Listen to the full session recording and follow along with the protocol. Rate how strongly you agree/disagree with each statement below. Make notes during the recording for accuracy.

---

### **Question 1**

**Statement:** The group leader followed the study protocol and delivered the required content.  
**Grading Scale:**  
A. Entirely Disagree - 1  
B. Mostly Disagree - 2  
C. Somewhat Disagree - 3  
D. Neither Agree nor Disagree - 4  
E. Somewhat Agree - 5  
F. Mostly Agree - 6  
G. Entirely Agree - 7

**What to Look For:**

- Completion of all main protocol sections.
- Minor phrasing/timing deviations are acceptable, but critical content must be covered.

---

**Scoring Key:**

- **1–3:** Low adherence/skill.

- **5–7:** High adherence/skill.
